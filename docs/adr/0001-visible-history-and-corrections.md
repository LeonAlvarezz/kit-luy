# Use Visible History and Corrections for Ledger Changes

Kit Luy records shared money events in a group chat where trust depends on members being able to explain why balances changed. We decided that purchases, repayment claims, repayments, and fixes remain visible in group history, and mistakes are handled through corrections or voids rather than silent edits or deletion. This makes the ledger more auditable and easier to dispute, at the cost of a more explicit history model than a simple mutable balance table.
